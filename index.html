People know me as a talkative, oversharing person who won’t shut up. I’ll stand right in front of you or sit beside you and start talking non-stop whenever something happens or whenever I know something. But sometimes I go quiet because there’s nothing to talk about — though I still listen closely when others share how their day went.

Once you get to know me better, you’ll notice this: when I’m talking or laughing, my close friend usually “takes the hit” — because when I laugh, I can’t help but slap her back or shake her, and I just can’t stop myself (≧∇≦). That’s just one of my love languages!

I’m also quite avoidant. When something feels overwhelming or pressuring, I’ll step away, then come back later as if nothing happened — no explanation needed.
