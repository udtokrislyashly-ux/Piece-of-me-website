I’m a talkative and oversharing person who won’t shut up whenever something happens in my day (✿◡‿◡). Sometimes, I’m a quiet person because I get lazy to socialize with my friends. I’ll just sit beside them, watch what they’re doing, or scroll through my phone.

Oh, my boyfriend knows how talkative I am, especially when we’re chatting through the phone. I only become talkative when I’m comfortable with someone, which is usually my family, friends, and that one man who lives far away, so I can only talk to him online (｡>︿<｡).

Also, I love annoying them until they get annoyed or angry ꉂ(ˊᗜˋ*). I guess that’s just one of my little ways of showing how comfortable and close I am with them.

Once you get to know me better, you’ll notice this: whenever I’m talking or laughing, my close friends usually “take the hit” — because when I laugh, I can’t help but lightly slap their arm or shake them, and I just can’t stop myself (≧∇≦). That’s just one of my ways of showing affection!

I’m also a bit avoidant. When something feels overwhelming or stressful, I tend to step away for a while and come back later as if nothing happened — sometimes without any explanation needed.

I’m a total papa’s girl — that’s what Mama always tells me because my mouth is always saying, “Where’s Papa, Ma?” or “Where are you going, Pa?” It’s always about Papa. Mama even jokes that I stole her man (＞ｙ＜).

Whenever I’m having a hard time or having a good day, I always tell them about it. That’s what they taught me: to share both the happy moments and the difficult ones with them.
